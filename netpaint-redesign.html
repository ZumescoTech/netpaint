Got Questions?

Frequently Asked
Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us directly and we'll get back to you within 4 hours.

How much does it cost to paint a house in Cape Town? +

Painting costs in Cape Town vary depending on the size of your home, surface condition, paint quality, and number of coats required. A typical 3-bedroom interior starts from R12,000–R18,000 fully inclusive. We always provide a detailed written quote after our free site visit so you know exactly what you're paying for.

How much does it cost to paint a house in Cape Town? +

A standard 3-bedroom house interior takes 3–5 working days. Exterior work and larger homes may take longer. We'll always give you a precise timeline in your written quote so you can plan accordingly.

Why are some painting quotes much cheaper than others? +

Cheaper quotes often mean less preparation work, cheaper paint, fewer coats, or unqualified painters. These shortcuts lead to peeling, discolouration, and costly repaints within 1–2 years. NetPaint uses only Dulux and Plascon premium paints and charges fairly for doing the job right the first time.

What is included in your painting service? +

Every NetPaint job includes furniture covering and protection, full surface preparation (sanding, crack filling, priming), 2–3 coats of premium paint, clean-up, and a final walkthrough with you before we leave. Colour consultation is also included free of charge.

How long does a painting project take? +

Timeline depends on the size and scope of the job. We provide a firm timeline in every written quote. We never rush — we'd rather take an extra day and deliver a result you'll love for years.

Do you handle preparation before painting? +

Yes — surface preparation is the most important part of any paint job and is fully included in every quote. We sand, fill cracks, apply primer, and treat any damp or mould before a single drop of paint goes on the wall.

Are you insured? Can I trust your team in my home? +

Yes. NetPaint is fully insured and all our painters are vetted, trained employees — not day labourers. We're also registered with relevant industry bodies. Your home and belongings are safe in our hands.

Do you help with choosing paint colour? +

Absolutely — our certified colour consultants offer a free consultation with every quote. We'll bring swatches, discuss your style and lighting, and help you choose a palette you'll love.

What paint brand works best for houses in Cape Town? +

We primarily use Dulux and Plascon — both are proven for Cape Town's coastal climate. Dulux Weathershield is our go-to for exteriors due to its superior UV and moisture resistance. We'll recommend the right product for your specific application.

Do you offer a warranty on your work? +

Yes. All NetPaint work comes with a written workmanship guarantee. Exterior paint jobs using Dulux Weathershield carry a 5-year paint guarantee. If anything fails to meet our standard, we'll come back and fix it.

How do I get a quote for my painting project? +

Simply fill in our online quote form, call us on 021 000 1234, or WhatsApp us on 082 123 4567. We'll visit your property at a time that suits you and provide a fully itemised written quote within 24 hours — at no cost and with no obligation.
