Service 03 · Surface Repairs

Surface Repairs, Damp & Crack Treatment

Paint hides nothing — cracks, damp and peeling come straight back unless the surface is properly fixed first. Our repair team treats the cause, not the symptom, so your new paint job lasts as long as it should.

  • ✓Damp diagnosis, treatment & rising-damp solutions.
  • ✓Crack stitching, plaster repair & filler work.
  • ✓Mould treatment with anti-fungal primers.
